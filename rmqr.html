About Rectangular Micro QR Codes

rMQR codes are a new rectangular variant of QR codes designed for space-constrained applications.

Space Efficient

Perfect for narrow spaces where traditional QR codes won't fit, with aspect ratios from 1:6 to 1:8.

Industrial Use

Ideal for small product packaging, pharmaceutical labels, and industrial component tracking.

ISO Standard

Fully compliant with ISO/IEC 23941:2022 standard for rectangular micro QR codes.
