Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about QR codes and our generator.

NexQR supports all standard QR code types including URL, plain text, contact information (vCard), email, SMS, phone numbers, WiFi credentials, and more. We also offer specialized QR codes like Micro QR and rMQR for specific use cases.

No, there are no limits! NexQR is completely free to use with no restrictions on the number of QR codes you can generate. All processing happens in your browser, so we don't track or limit your usage.

Our generator automatically checks the scanability of your QR code as you customize it. We recommend using higher error correction levels (M or H) when adding logos or colors, maintaining sufficient contrast between dark and light modules, and testing your final QR code with multiple scanning apps.

You can download your QR codes in PNG (raster) format for most uses, or SVG (vector) format for high-quality printing and scaling. Some specialized QR types also support PDF download for professional printing purposes.

NexQR focuses on static QR code generation. For tracking capabilities, we recommend using a URL shortener service with analytics (like Bitly) before generating your QR code. Simply create a trackable shortened URL first, then generate a QR code for that URL using NexQR.
