Why Use Micro QR Codes?

Micro QR codes offer unique advantages for specific use cases where space is limited.

Compact Size

Micro QR codes are up to 30% smaller than standard QR codes, perfect for tiny product labels and packaging.

Limited Data

Ideal for simple numeric or alphanumeric data like serial numbers, product IDs, or short URLs.

Industrial Use

Widely used in manufacturing, logistics, and inventory systems where space is at a premium.
